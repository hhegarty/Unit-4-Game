Crystal Collector

How to play

You'll be given a random number at the start of the game.There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to the random number. However, you lose if your total score goes above the random number

Value of each crystal is hidden until clicked upon.Each time when the game starts the values of the crystals will change
