Rare Skull Crystals Collector

How to play

You're given a random number below. Clicking on a skull will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The Value of each skull is hidden until clicked upon. Each time when the game starts the values of the skulls will change.
